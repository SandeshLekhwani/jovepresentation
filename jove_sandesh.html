Initiative 1: JoVE AI Lab Mentor — The Opportunity

The Problem

Static video leads to passive learning. Students and early-career researchers need contextual, on-demand help to understand complex protocols, and professors lack tools to track comprehension.

The Vision

Transform passive viewing into an active learning dialogue. Create an intelligent AI-powered layer over JoVE's entire video library that acts as a personal, 24/7 lab mentor.

JoVE AI Lab Mentor: Path to Commercialization

Phase 1: MVP & Pilot

  • Distribution: Free pilot with 5-10 partner university labs.
  • Metrics: WAU, Queries/Session, Qualitative Feedback.

Phase 2: Beta & LMS Integration

  • Distribution: Market as a beta feature via LMS integrations (Canvas, Moodle).
  • Metrics: LMS Adoption %, Student Performance Lift, NPS.

Phase 3: Commercial Launch

  • Distribution: Package as a premium add-on to institutional subscriptions.
  • Metrics: New ARR, Upsell Conversion Rate, Churn Reduction.

Initiative 2: JoVE Research Nexus — The Opportunity

The Problem

Scientific research is increasingly interdisciplinary, but content platforms are siloed. Researchers struggle to discover relevant techniques from other fields, and collaboration happens on disconnected platforms.

The Vision

Create a hub for cross-disciplinary discovery and collaboration. Enable research labs to create shared knowledge bases around JoVE's content, breaking down silos and accelerating innovation.

JoVE Research Nexus: Path to Commercialization

Phase 1: MVP (Collections)

  • Distribution: Promote "Technique Playlists" to existing users via email/homepage.
  • Metrics: CTR, Cross-Discipline Views, Session Duration.

Phase 2: Beta (Workspaces)

  • Distribution: Invite PIs at key institutions to pilot private lab workspaces.
  • Metrics: # Active Workspaces, Annotations, User Retention.

Phase 3: Commercial Launch

  • Distribution: Offer a "Lab/Team" license tier sold directly to PIs & department heads.
  • Metrics: New ARR, Market Penetration %, User Growth.
